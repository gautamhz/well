What this service covers

Websites that look good and do the job.

At Colours and Patterns, we design and build websites for businesses in Guwahati and across India that look the part - and more importantly, actually work. Corporate sites, e-commerce stores, portfolios, and lead-generation landing pages all start with experience, SEO, and conversion.

The platform gets picked based on what fits the project: WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, or custom builds when nothing off the shelf will cut it.

Speed matters a lot. Mobile matters even more, because most of your traffic is probably already on a phone. The design focuses on clean navigation, clear hierarchy, and CTAs that do not make a first-time visitor hunt for what to do next.

On the technical side, we pay attention to Core Web Vitals, structured data, schema, and on-page SEO so Google has every reason to send traffic your way.

A website alone is not the full digital presence. Google Business Profile, Linktree, blog content, analytics, and online brand assets all need to work together so the brand is easier to find, easier to trust, and easier to pick.

What we do

  • Corporate websites, e-commerce stores, portfolios, and landing pages
  • WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, or custom builds based on project need
  • Mobile-first UX, clean navigation, clear hierarchy, and stronger CTA flow
  • Core Web Vitals, structured data, schema, and on-page SEO
  • Google Business Profile, Linktree, blog content, analytics, and online assets
